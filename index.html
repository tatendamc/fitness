📐 The 3-Phase Plan

  • Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Foundation — rebuild movement patterns, build base cardio, light weights. Focus on form over load.
  • Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8): Build — increase weight, add supersets, push cardio intensity. Your body is adapted now.
  • Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12): Peak — heavier loads, circuit training, HIIT cardio. Arrive in Zimbabwe feeling strong.

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Warm up 5 min on treadmill/bike before every session
  • Start with lighter dumbbells than you think you need
  • If anything hurts (not muscle burn, actual pain), stop
  • Rest 60-90s between sets (Phase 1), 45-60s (Phase 2-3)
  • Hydrate — aim for 2-3L water on training days
  • This is general fitness guidance, not medical advice. Listen to your body.

🍽️ Simple Nutrition Tips

  • Protein with every meal (chicken, fish, eggs, beans)
  • Eat within 1 hour after training
  • Cut sugary drinks — water, black coffee, tea
  • Don't starve yourself — you need fuel to build muscle
